Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your accommodations sustainable?
Our eco-domes are 100% solar-powered, using grid-tied solar panels to provide all electricity needs. We've painted them in native tussock olive to blend with the landscape, and our building materials were chosen for minimal environmental impact while maintaining luxury standards.
How close is the cycle trail really?
The Cromwell to Clyde cycle trail starts exactly 300 meters from your accommodation - a 3-minute walk. You can literally step out your door, walk down our lane, and be on one of New Zealand's most scenic cycle trails. No transport needed!
What's included with breakfast at the domes?
Continental breakfast for two includes fresh local bread, preserves, cereals, yogurt, fruit, coffee, tea, and juice. We focus on Central Otago produce where possible. Everything is stocked in your dome's full kitchen before arrival.
Can we really see stars through the skylight?
Absolutely! Central Otago has some of New Zealand's darkest skies. The skylight in Dome Pinot is positioned directly above the bed with dimmable interior lighting. On clear nights, you'll see the Milky Way, Southern Cross, and countless stars. It's magical!
Are pets really welcome at the cottage?
Yes! The Lakeside Cottage is completely pet-friendly. We welcome well-behaved dogs and have our own friendly dogs on the property. The cottage has secure fencing and we provide dog bowls. Just let us know about your furry family members when booking.
