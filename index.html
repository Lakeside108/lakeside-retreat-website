OUR STORY

Where Lake Dunstan Meets Wine Country Magic

High above the pristine waters of Lake Dunstan, where ancient schist mountains meet rolling vineyard hills, lies a sanctuary that captures the very essence of Central Otago. Lakeside Retreat was born from a simple dream: to create a place where luxury meets sustainability, where modern comfort embraces timeless landscape.

Our accommodation offerings reflect this philosophy perfectly. Choose from our innovative grid-tied solar-powered eco-domes, painted in the soft olive of native tussock grass, or our luxury waterfront cottage with every modern amenity. Each morning, whether you wake in sustainable dome luxury or cottage comfort, you'll witness mist rising from the lake below while golden light dances across the surrounding peaks.

Just minutes from your door, world-renowned wineries invite you to taste the terroir that makes Central Otago Pinot Noir legendary. Yet at day's end, you'll return to your private haven, where the only sounds are lapping lake waters and the whisper of mountain winds through tussock land.

This is where your Central Otago story begins—choose sustainable dome luxury or full-service cottage comfort, both authentic, unforgettable, and perfectly positioned above Lake Dunstan.

ABOUT US

Your hosts, passionate guardians of Central Otago's natural beauty

Welcome to Our Lakeside Dream

We're more than just hosts—we're passionate advocates for Central Otago's extraordinary landscape and devoted guardians of its pristine environment. When we first discovered this breathtaking spot above Lake Dunstan, we knew we'd found something truly special.

As long-time residents of Central Otago, we've watched this region transform into one of the world's most celebrated wine destinations. Yet through all the growth and recognition, the raw beauty of schist mountains reflecting in crystal-clear lake waters has remained timeless and inspiring.

Our vision for Lakeside Retreat was born from a simple belief: that luxury travel should enhance, not exploit, the natural world. Every solar panel we installed, every sustainable feature we incorporated, and every detail we carefully considered reflects our commitment to preserving this magical place for future generations.

We take immense pride in sharing our corner of paradise with travelers who appreciate both luxury and responsibility. Whether you're here for the world-class wines, the stunning landscapes, or simply to disconnect and recharge, we're here to ensure your experience exceeds every expectation.
