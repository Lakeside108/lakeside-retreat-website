OUR STORY

Where Lake Dunstan Meets Wine Country Magic

High above the pristine waters of Lake Dunstan, where ancient schist mountains meet rolling vineyard hills, lies a sanctuary that captures the very essence of Central Otago. Lakeside Retreat was born from a simple dream: to create a place where luxury meets sustainability, where modern comfort embraces timeless landscape.

Our eco-luxury domes, painted in the soft olive of native tussock grass, seem to emerge naturally from the hillside. Each morning, you'll wake to mist rising from the lake below, while golden light dances across the surrounding peaks. This is more than accommodation—it's your gateway to one of New Zealand's most celebrated wine regions.

Just minutes from your door, world-renowned wineries invite you to taste the terroir that makes Central Otago Pinot Noir legendary. Yet at day's end, you'll return to your private haven, where the only sounds are lapping lake waters and the whisper of mountain winds through tussock land.

This is where your Central Otago story begins—authentic, sustainable, unforgettable.
